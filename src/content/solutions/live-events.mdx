## The Solution: 5G Bonding & SRT

We replace heavy OB Vans with a backpack. By aggregating multiple network connections (4G/5G, WiFi, Ethernet), CNDLive encoders create a single, robust pipeline for your video.

### How Portable Bonding Works

<Steps>

### 1. Signal Acquisition
Connect your camera directly to our portable encoder (like the **P-Series**) via SDI or HDMI.

### 2. Network Aggregation (Bonding)
The encoder splits the video data across multiple SIM cards and local networks simultaneously. If one carrier drops, the others pick up the slack instantly.

### 3. Reliable Transport
We use **SRT (Secure Reliable Transport)** protocol to recover lost packets over the public internet, ensuring a glitch-free feed even on jittery connections.

### 4. Cloud / Studio Production
The bonded signal lands in your studio or cloud production mixer (vMix, TriCaster) for graphics, replays, and distribution.

</Steps>
