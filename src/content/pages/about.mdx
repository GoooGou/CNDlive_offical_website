## Who We Are

**CNDLive** is a specialized subsidiary of **CND Electronics Technology Co., Ltd**, established in **2006** in Shenzhen.

CND Electronics has dedicated over a decade to the innovation and development of hardware and software in the **Smart Information Interactive Industry**. We excel in information display, intelligent dual-touch technology, biometric processing, and HD remote real-time transmission.

With this profound experience, CNDLive was born to focus specifically on **IP-based video and audio transmission**.

## Our Journey

From smart displays to professional streaming.
